The problem

The human override
has no oversight.

Every tool audits the AI algorithm. Nobody audits the human manager who overrides it.

When a manager rejects a candidate based on where they studied, vague cultural fit, or physical assumptions — that decision disappears. No record. No consequence.

Under the EU AI Act, that is a compliance violation. When enforcement begins, you have nothing to show the regulator.

EU AI ACT · ARTICLE 6 · HIGH-RISK AI SYSTEMS

AI systems used for recruitment are classified as high-risk. Organisations must maintain a detailed audit log of all AI-assisted decisions. Deadline: August 2, 2026.
